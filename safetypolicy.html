Safety Policy

OSHA 1994, SECTION 16

It is the duty of an employer to formulate a safety and health policy. Failing which, a fine upto RM50,000 may be imposed.

Generally, a Safety Policy is a written statement by the employer ensuring the safety, health, and well-being of all employees and visitors on the premises / work activities
